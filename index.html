    Visualizations

    The project describes the analysis of several cancer drugs within a mock trial with mice studies, survival rate and endpoint analysis. Refer to the plots for visualizations and conclusions from Data Analyis. Further dataset is also available in the data folder.

    Libraries Used:

    1. Pandas, Python to read, aggregate, analyze, and interpret the data from two CSV files.
    2. Matplotlib and Seaborn to build graphs and charts to show the results and analysis
